"Death is at your side" is a game specifically
("Kei tou taha te mate" he kemu motuhake tenei)
made for anyone looking for a quick and easy to understand game in their free time.
(hangaia mo te tangata e rapu ana i te keemu tere me te maarama ki o raatau waatea.)
Enjoy!
Kia pai!




*ctrl r to restart the game



Instructions

1. Use the UP and DOWN buttons to move the character up and down.

(Whakamahia te ki runga me te iho patene ki te neke i te tangata ki runga, ki raro hoki.)

2. Avoid the obstacles and try not to hit the sides!

(Aukati i nga aukati me te ngana kia kaua e pa ki nga taha!)

3. Survive, Strive, Remember, DEATH IS AT YOUR SIDE...

(Ora, Ngana, Mahara, Ko te mate kei to taha ...)
